Crepes

This recipe takes just an hour, but trust me—you’ll be having so much fun mixing, flipping,
and tasting that time will fly by! Before you know it, you’ll have a golden stack of warm, delicate
crepes ready to be filled with anything your heart (or sweet tooth) desires. Whether you're craving
something fruity, chocolatey, or savory, these crepes are the perfect blank canvas. Light, buttery, and
perfectly thin—they’re the kind of treat that makes you say, “just one more…”

Engredients

  • eggs - 3
  • sugar - 1tbsp
  • salt - 1tsp
  • milk - 500ml
  • flour - 280g
  • oil - 3tbsp

Recipe

  1. At first take a big bowl. Then mix eggs, sugar and salt using whisk.
    Don't do it for too long and puting too much effort in it: main thing is to
    ingridients to connect together.


    3. In the recieved egg mixture, base of crepes pour half of cold milk. Stir with
    a whisk until a homogrnrous mass is obtained. Sift the flour through a fine
    sieve,     but don't add it yet.


  3. Little by little start adding sifted flour into the egg-milk mixture, stirring with
    whisk or spatula until a homogeneous mass similar to sour cream is obtained. Make sure
    there are no lumps.


  4. Into the resulting dough add the remaining milk and mix thoroughly with a whisk.
    Pour in vegetable oil. Mix and leave for 10-15 minutes at room temperature.


  5. Heat a cast-iron frying pan, grease with vegetable oil and fry crepes on
    both sides. Lay them out in a stack, smearing with a small piece of butter.


  6. Finally! Now you can eat those tasty crepes!
    (if you don't ate them while were cooking...)
