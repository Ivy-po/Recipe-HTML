Crepes

Tasty crepes, easy recipe! This recipe takes an hour but you will not
even notice how it pass and you have crepes!

  • eggs - 3
  • sugar - 1tbsp
  • salt - 1tsp
  • milk - 500ml
  • flour - 280g
  • oil - 3tbsp

Recipe

  1. At first take a big bowl. Then lightly mix eggs, sugar and salt using whisk.
    Don't do it for too long and puting too much effort in it: main thing is to
    ingridients to connect together.

    3. In the recieved egg mixture, base of crepes put half of cold milk. Stir with
    a whisk until a homogrnrous mass is obtained. Sift the flour through a fine
    sieve, but don't add it yet.

  3. Little by little start adding sifted flour into the egg-milk mixture, stirring with
    whisk or spatula until a homogeneous mass similar to sour cream is obtained. Make sure
    there are no lumps.

  4. Into the resulting dough add the remaining milk and mix thoroughly with a whisk. Pour in
    vegetable oil. Mix and leave for 10-15 minutes at room temperature.
